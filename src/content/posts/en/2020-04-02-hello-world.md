## FE

I'm also doing some steady progress on my FE (Fundamental Information Technology Engineer) studies, starting by skimming through a reference book.

I know it would probably be better to summarize the key points in a notebook or solve the practice exercises, but because I want knowledge rather than a certification, I'm using that as an excuse to study inefficiently.

I'm not exactly good at sticking with long-term study, and if I lose my motivation, it’s all for nothing. So, at my current level, I think it's better to just study in the way that I want to.

## IIDX

- New HARD clears
- New EXH clears

I got the HARD clear on IX fairly easily. I had been aiming for it periodically and figured I would get it eventually without needing to obsessively grind for it, and that's exactly what happened. I think I had enough skill; I was just waiting for the right note density and a moment of focus.

I think the fact that the arrangement ended up being a 1P split, which was likely favorable, was also a factor in getting it so easily.

GENE is a song I really like, and strictly speaking, it felt like it was just within the range of an EXH clear, so when I realized it was possible, I got quite nervous. It subtly has nearly 2,200 notes, so it’s a bit heavy. It’s truly a great song with a great chart.

Updating my data on CPI and IST has become part of my daily routine lately, but it's a bit of a hassle, so I'm thinking about automating it with something like AWS Lambda + Puppeteer.
