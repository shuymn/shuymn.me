I watched "[Wandering Witch: The Journey of Elaina](https://majotabi.jp/)" on d Anime Store.

I didn't know the original light novels beforehand, but I discovered it while channel surfing when the Fall 2020 anime season started. The atmosphere seemed nice, so I decided to watch it.

It’s a story about a protagonist traveling through various countries with different worldviews, much like *Kino's Journey*.

It wasn't bad, but it gave me the impression of being *Kino's Journey* mixed with the trends of recent light novels. I wouldn't stop anyone from watching it if they wanted to, but I'm not sure I would actively recommend it to others.

I have a tendency to rate inconsistent works lower, and regarding this series, I wasn't sure until the end whether I should be viewing it as a comedy or as a serious drama.
