I purchased this at the end of last year.

Since it was over the New Year holidays, I expected it to arrive in February, but it actually arrived in mid-January.

The purchase email said "We will notify you when it has shipped," but I hadn't received a shipping notice when it arrived, so its sudden arrival was a surprise.

I kept all customizations at their default settings since I can customize it however I like later on.

I've been using it for about half a month now and I'm very satisfied; I think it was a good purchase.

Here is my current customization setup:

- Buttons
    - Default (DAO buttons). Basic sound dampening already applied.
- Microswitches
    - Replaced with Omron D2MV-01-1C3 (0.49N)
- Springs
    - Replaced with Sanwa Denshi OBSA-SP-50g (50g)
- Turntable
    - It felt too light, so I added felt to the back of the turntable to increase friction.

I have a feeling that explaining the specific sound dampening methods would be long due to the trial and error involved, so I plan to post about it in a separate article if I feel up to writing it.
